Curriculum


The University of Egeria is committed to providing quality education through the Explicit Curriculum that made to facilitate effective learning. The curriculum is structured in small, feasible steps that allow students to master each lesson before moving on to the next. This approach ensures that students have a solid foundation of knowledge and can keep up with the pace of the course. Moreover, the University closely monitors each student's progress to ensure that they are successfully participating and understanding the material.
