FINGERPRINTS OF FREEDOM

AA 3 - Sonnet Writing

Our poems encompass the theme of freedom. Freedom takes many forms, like breaking free from toxic relationships, protesting against oppression, or simply the hope to live freely. Like a fingerprint, each of us has a unique interpretation of freedom, but we share a common aspiration for independence. We aim to capture these emotions through our sonnets.
